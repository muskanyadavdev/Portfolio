CampaignDesk

Email-marketing SaaS
Full-stack build
[ your role: solo, or which parts were yours? ]
Live ↗ Code ↗

The problem

Teams running email campaigns juggle contacts, templates, sending, and results across disconnected tools — and still can't easily see what happened to each email. CampaignDesk puts the entire loop in one app: build an audience, design a template, send, and track every message.

What I built

A reusable template library with merge variables — {{name}}, {{otp}}, {{discount}} — for per-recipient personalization, feeding a guided three-step campaign builder: choose audience → pick template → review and send.

A delivery-tracking dashboard that logs each message and surfaces live, filterable status — delivered, bounced, pending — with per-recipient detail and CSV export, so a campaign's outcome is visible at a glance.

Account security through JWT authentication with OTP email verification and role-based access control (Super Admin / Admin), backed by a FastAPI service, a PostgreSQL schema, and Alembic migrations.

A LinkedIn integration layer: OAuth publishing, analytics sync, one-click conversion of an email template into a LinkedIn post, scheduled auto-posting, and Slack / Teams webhook alerts on send events.

The hard part

The most involved piece was the delivery-tracking pipeline — capturing per-recipient results, reconciling bounced and pending states, and surfacing them as live filterable data — closely followed by the LinkedIn OAuth flow and template-to-post conversion.
